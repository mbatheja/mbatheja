## Hi there, I am Mahima 👋

I build data-driven healthcare products that make clinical and health data more accessible to patients, providers, and healthcare stakeholders. Most of my work sits at the intersection of data science, machine learning, and product thinking, with a strong focus on real-world impact.

My toolkit includes: Python, SQL, PySpark, Databricks, Apache Airflow, Dbt, ArcGIS Pro, Docker, FastAPI and ML/NLP workflows.

Let’s collaborate on: Machine Learning, NLP, healthcare analytics, data platforms, or data-backed product prototypes.
